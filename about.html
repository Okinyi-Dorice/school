HISTORY

Brief History. Moi University was established in 1984 by an Act of Parliament (Moi University Act, 1984)
as the second public university in Kenya. This was on the recommendation of a Presidential Working Party, chaired by Prof.
Collins B. Mackay, which had collected views from Kenyans about the desirability of the same.
